Who We Are

An academy built like a startup studio, not a classroom.

Unlike traditional business schools that teach theory, Artistic School of Business (ASB) focuses on building real businesses, brands and market-ready ventures.

The academy is designed as a business creation lab where education meets execution, preparing students for the realities of the modern market. We're built and operated by The Artistic Company — which means our curriculum is shaped by people who are running businesses right now.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Master in Entrepreneurial Business Building is a 6-month, full-time intensive, structured across 8 phases from idea to investor pitch. Our shorter programs run 1–4 months for focused skill-building.

We don't teach business theory in isolation — we build real ventures with you. Every student leaves with a working brand, product, and pitch, not just a certificate.

ASB is built and operated by The Artistic Company, so mentors are active entrepreneurs and industry leaders running businesses today, not just academic faculty.

Yes. Phase 8 of the flagship program is a live Investor Pitch on Demo Day, where founders present to 50+ VCs and past graduates have secured follow-up meetings and term sheets.
